New micro snail found

26-3-25: A new micro-snail species, Clostophis udayaditinus, was recently found in karsts from Phnom Banan in Battambang province. The species is named in honour of King Udayadityavarman II (1050-1066), the first king to build Phnom Banan Temple, after which the hill was named.


Prior to the study, 13 species from five genera of terrestrial snails had been recorded in Karst from Cambodia, but no species from the genus Clostophis has ever been documented.

- Khmer Times

Dying linked to shorter lifespans

Researchers at Sajiwa Central Jail have linked death with shorter lifespans. The researchers believe that stopping of the heart is not the best for health, both physically and mentally. Additionally, the death of the brain can cause its deterioration over time.
